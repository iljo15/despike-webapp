About Despike

Our Mission

Despike was created to simplify the process of analyzing thermal expansion data. Our platform provides researchers and engineers with powerful tools to visualize, process, and understand complex datasets.

What We Do

Our web application specializes in processing thermal expansion coefficient (CTE) data at different heating rates. We provide visualization tools, data smoothing algorithms, and exportable results for further analysis.
